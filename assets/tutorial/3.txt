Ice Defense - ups your defense
for a limited time

Water Spike - click to raise a
bunch of water spikes that do damage
to whoever is on top of it
Water Darts - sends a small but fast
dart out the direction you're facing

Water Blade - sends a big, slower,
but more powerful water blast
the direction you're facing
Water's Embrace - gives you healing
at a slower pace but follows you around

Whirlpool - creates a giant whirlpool
around you that deals a lot of damage
Water - creates a river in the direction
you clicked in that disappears after
a long time
Dead Calm - locks you in place but also
stops all incoming attacks
