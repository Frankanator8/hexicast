Boomerang - launches a boomerang
to where you click, then returns
to the player's current position

Earth Wall - creates a permanent wall
in front of you that can block low spells
Shield - creates a temporary shield
in front of you that can block low spells
Footsteps - gives you a hint in the
direction of the closest player

Boulder - acts as a 3-boulder shotgun.
Loses damage with time
Rocket Launch - temporarily boosts you
up into the air

Falling Mountains - summon a bunch of
falling mountains where you click
Sandstorm - create a sandstorm to confuse
your opponents on where you are
